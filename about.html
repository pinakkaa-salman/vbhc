Since its inception, Value Based Housing Corporation (VBHC) has been a visionary force in the ever-evolving real estate industry. Our brand aspires to lead a revolution spearheaded by time-tested values of Integrity, Transparency, and Excellence.

Residences crafted by VBHC are an embodiment of these core values. Through sharp strategic planning, ground-breaking designs, and state-of-the-art technology, we create architectural wonders that transcend existing benchmarks and set new ones. Our thoughtfully designed 3 BHK flats in gated communities offer security, serenity and sophistication.

With a customer-centric approach, VBHC envisions living environments that seamlessly blend luxury with sustainability. Every project we actualise is a testament to our innovative spirit, offering 3 BHK flats with modern amenities that redefine comfort and convenience for today’s homeowners.

In our endeavour to create timeless constructions, we have been steadily backed by prominent investors such as HDFC, IFC, AMBIT, and Van Herk Group, reinforcing our commitment to quality and trust.

VBHC was a vision brought to life by pioneers Jaithirth (Jerry) Rao and P. S. Jayakumar with the sole purpose of building township projects in key Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, and Chennai. In the process of delivering 7200+ homes over the years, VBHC built an illustrious legacy for itself on the foundation of dependability, quality, and client satisfaction. VBHC’s pursuit of perfection enabled us to develop an excellent reputation that was revered by peers and trusted by the public.

The trust bestowed on us by the people has empowered us to venture into an avenue that they aspire to - the ultra-luxury real estate sector. VBHC plans to navigate this dynamic landscape by adhering to the core principles of Integrity, Transparency, and Excellence. We aim to revolutionise this segment with innovative homes that blend Elegance, Creativity, and Sustainability.
